Agent Profile

Anup Rai is a professional real estate agent with over 25 years of realty experience in Ontario. Anup specializes in finding properties in prime locations, and has a network of specialists to turn your house into a dream home. His approach to real estate is to understand your situation and to find a property that will work for you now and for the long term. Anup has experience selling in remote towns in Ontario, and can package renovation projects to offer a turnkey experience for you as well. He also has extensive experience in the Golden Horseshoe area and the areas with the most value to find a property there. Ask Anup about opportunities for turnkey tiny house real estate or mobile homes as well. He will connect you with the right parties to ensure success. He is the proud father of three children, and spends his weekends hiking with his dogs in the Hamilton Escarpment.
